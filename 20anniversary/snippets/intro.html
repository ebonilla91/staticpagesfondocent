Embracing our roots and weaving the present to materialize the future

Welcome to our micro-site, a space created with love whose purpose is honoring the path we have walked for over twenty years as a women’s fund in Central America.

In this space, you’ll get to know the purpose of our existence and accompany us on a journey through our process of growth, exploration, and reinvention. We, for our part, will share with you what drives and sustains our work model and the compass that guides our actions as we move toward the change we desire so dearly.

We will also tell you inspiring stories of organizations that are generating powerful changes in their communities and courageously resisting the system via political creativity and collective action. We will also present you with data that illustrates the impact of our commitment in these twenty years, and we will conclude by recognizing those who have contributed to our continued existence.
